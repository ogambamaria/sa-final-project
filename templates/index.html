VADER Sentiment Analysis

How to interpret the results:

- The compound score falls in the range -1 to 1.

- If the compound score > 0, the sentiments are mostly positive. The closer it is to 1, the more positive the sentiments.

- If the compound score < 0, the sentiments are mostly negative. The closer it is to -1, the more negative the sentiments.
